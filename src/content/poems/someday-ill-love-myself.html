Someday I’ll Love Myself

Not today.

The fall of the oceans rise to drown me,

 

and I’ll never escape the

tantalizing self-frustration.

 

INADEQUATE and

worthless.

 

A cold midsummer day entangles

the night and the darkness

 

that is

my mind.

my soul, my body

Hearkens to a truth.

Raindrops rise.

A warm shadow

drifts

 

Alone and loathed,

by himself
